Professional Statement

As a dedicated psychology researcher with extensive experience in academic and industry settings, I am passionate about advancing our understanding of human behavior, emotion regulation, and cognitive processes. My research focuses on the intersection of mindfulness-based interventions and emotion regulation, with particular interest in how these findings can be applied to improve mental health outcomes.

Through my work at the University of Amsterdam, University of Zagreb, and Vitafluence.ai, I have developed expertise in meta-analysis, systematic literature reviews, and digital biomarker research. I am particularly interested in the potential of AI-driven solutions to enhance psychological assessment and intervention strategies.

My international experience across Croatia, Netherlands, and Germany has provided me with a global perspective on psychological research and its applications. I am committed to conducting rigorous, evidence-based research that contributes meaningfully to the field of psychology and ultimately benefits individuals and communities.
